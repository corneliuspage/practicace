Why PracticAce

PracticAce offers a comprehensive lab manual with WAEC standard practicals, a basic lab kit that is sufficient to carry out every experiment in the manual and videos to guide you while doing the experiments. In addition, if you still have questions or need explanation on anything related to the lab experiments, there are comment sections under each video where you can ask and get answers from many other users, including team members at PracticAce.
